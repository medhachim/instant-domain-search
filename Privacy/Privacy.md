Privacy Policy for Instant Domain Search Pro
Last Updated: May 15, 2026

1. Introduction
Instant Domain Search Pro ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data Collection

No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, location, or IP address).

No Browsing History: We do not track or monitor your browsing history or the websites you visit.

Search Queries: The domain names you type into the search bar are sent directly to our API provider (APIFreaks) solely to check for availability and provide suggestions. These queries are processed in real-time and are not stored by the extension.

3. Use of Permissions

Storage: Used only to save your preferences and settings locally within your browser for a better user experience.

Host Permission: Required to communicate with https://api.apifreaks.com to fetch domain status.

4. Third-Party Services
Our extension provides affiliate links to domain registrars (e.g., Namecheap). If you click on these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to read.

5. Data Security
Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information stored on our servers to protect. All API communications are conducted over secure HTTPS connections.

6. Compliance
We comply with the Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, including the "Single Purpose" and "User Data" policies.
